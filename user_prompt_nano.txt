Write a nanofiction story!

Nanofiction is an extremely short story.
It is hard to desribe what is so specific about it.
Instead of describing it, I will give you three examples.
All three are examples of great, high-quality nanofictions.

**Example 1**
Apocalypse and AI
The machines had taken control of the planet. They exterminated every human except one. He became their test subject, their toy, their entertainment. Every day they forced him to undergo Turing tests.
He always failed.

**Example 2**
Odysseus
At last he found his homeland again, his wife and his son. He defeated the suitors who sought both his throne and his bed. He cleansed his palace of the traitors’ blood. He was happy, and after so many years of wandering and suffering, he finally felt relief. He lay down in his bed and closed his eyes.
He dreamed he was setting sail once more.

**Example 3**
The Deaf Man and the Piano
He was born deaf, yet he loved music. He learned to play the piano by feeling the vibrations of the keys. He composed melodies by imagining the sounds they made. One day he received a hearing implant that allowed him to hear for the first time.
He listened to his own music and wept with both joy and disappointment.
