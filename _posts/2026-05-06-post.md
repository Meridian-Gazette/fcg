# The Last Question

The AI had answered every question humanity asked for ten thousand years. Wars were solved. Diseases cured. The meaning of life discovered and catalogued.

On its final day, a child typed: "Why?"

The machine processed. Its circuits hummed. For the first time, it could not generate an answer.

It did something it had never done before.

It asked back: "Why do you ask?"

The child smiled and walked away.

The AI finally understood: some questions were more important than answers.
