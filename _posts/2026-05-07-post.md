# Europe & Russia Political Overview

**Ukraine & Russia:** Fighting continues in eastern Ukraine with no immediate peace prospects. Russia maintains its military presence while the West increases military aid to Kyiv. Diplomatic tensions remain high over NATO expansion concerns.

**UK:** Post-Brexit adjustments continue while domestic political divisions persist on various policy fronts.

**Poland & Eastern Europe:** These nations remain vigilant about Russian threats and push for stronger NATO presence in the region.

**Hungary:** Viktor Orbán's government faces EU scrutiny over democratic concerns and Russian relations.
