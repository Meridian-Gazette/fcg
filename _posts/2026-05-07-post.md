**The Translator**

He spent forty years translating the works of a foreign poet into his own language. He sacrificed everything — career, friendships, health — to preserve every nuance, every silence between the words. Critics called his translations masterpieces, perhaps superior to the originals. He became famous, celebrated, invited to speak at universities worldwide.

One evening, alone in his study, he opened a blank notebook and tried to write something of his own.

The page remained empty until morning.
